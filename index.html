self thaught anime illustration projects

This is a collection of my illustrations. My style is inspired by the japanese aesthetic, as well as chinese and korean visual cultures.

3D Modeling

3d modelling, texturing, rigging & rendering

Some 3D projects made by me where i model, texture, rig and render everything.
I start by looking up blueprints, giving them volume next. After the model is to my liking I make the textures or use available ones and next rig everything if needed. Rendering is done on the highest quality settings and then retouched in image-editing software.
